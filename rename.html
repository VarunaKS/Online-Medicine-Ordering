our story

This website was an attempt to reach out and make the avaliability of medicines much more easy and simple to the general public and to help them get easy access to medicines.
Through this website we hope we have reached all those who cannot actually go walking out to the store to buy the required medicines and hope that we have it made it easier for them to purchase and stock up on the medicines they require.
This hope and attempt to help the public is what gave birth to this website:

"HELPING HANDS"

that helps deliver the medicines required to the general public.
We hope that the public is really happy and pleased with our services and will continue to rely on us for their future medical requirements.

We always endeavour to help you out.



values & mission

through the creation of this app we hope to achieve the following:

  1. easier access to medicines
  2. faster access to the medicines
  3. notify the general public about any and all discounts on medicines
  4. help them buy even the most rarest of medicines
  5. help in quick and fast delivery of medicines
